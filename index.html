Discover Eternal Hope / Life

You are not here by accident. God has a message of love, forgiveness, and new life just for you.
Are you seeking truth and purpose in life? Do you yearn for a deeper connection with God? Jesus Christ is the way, the truth, and the life. Through Him, you can experience forgiveness, redemption, and a personal relationship with the Creator.

The Urgency of Decision:
" Today, we're faced with a crucial decision that can determine our eternal destiny. The Bible reminds us that 'now is the time of God's favor, now is the day of salvation' (2 Corinthians 6:2). We don't know when our time on this earth will come to an end, but we do know that we should be prepared to meet our Lord at any moment.

The Uncertainty of Life:
The Bible warns us that 'man proposes, but God disposes' (Proverbs 19:21). Our lives can change in an instant, and we may not have tomorrow to make a decision. As the apostle Paul wrote, 'We are not to live in the shadow of tomorrow, but in the light of today' (Hebrews 3:13, paraphrased). Why not make the decision to accept Christ now, so that when our time comes, we can rest assured that we are safe in His loving arms?

The Consequences of Rejection:
The Bible teaches us that 'the wages of sin is death, but the gift of God is eternal life in Christ Jesus our Lord' (Romans 6:23). If we reject Christ, we risk facing eternal separation from God, which is described as 'burning hellfire' (Matthew 25:41, Revelation 20:15). This is a reality we should all take seriously.

The Benefits of Acceptance:
On the other hand, accepting Christ brings us into a loving relationship with God, where we can experience true fulfillment and joy. As Jesus said, 'I have come that they may have life, and have it to the full' (John 10:10). In Christ, we find the peace, love, and purpose that we were created for. Brethren, note that; "there is not fulfilment in the world but only in Christ."

God's Willingness to Accept Us:
The good news is that God is willing to accept us just as we are. He loves us with an everlasting love and desires to forgive us and restore us to a right relationship with Him (Jeremiah 31:3, 1 John 1:9). As the Bible says, 'Whoever comes to me I will never drive away' (John 6:37).

A Call to Decision:
So, why not make the decision to accept Christ now? Let's not wait any longer to experience the joy, peace, and fulfillment that comes from knowing Him. As the Bible says, 'Today, if you hear his voice, do not harden your hearts' (Hebrews 3:15). Let's accept Christ now and secure our place in eternity.

Dear Reader, I want you to ask yourself this question:
"If I die today, will I end up in Heaven or Hell?
And if I miss Heaven, you can bet I won't miss Hell!"
